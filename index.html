Ginna Ericksen

Candidate for Board of Education

Wheaton Warrenville School District 200

As a 25 year Wheaton resident, I have a long running interest in District 200. My four children attended Wiesbrook, Hubble (old and new) and Wheaton Warrenville South and I appreciate the education and life preparation that they received from District 200. I am running for the School Board as a way to pay it forward by ensuring that we continue to provide a quality educational experience for the children of our community.
