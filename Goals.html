Ginna Ericksen, Candidate for Board of Education

I Believe

  • District 200's biggest strength is its talented and dedicated staff.
  • We have a strong parent network supporting the efforts of our educators.
  • Our School Board has worked hard to provide an outstanding program to our children while acting as a thoughtful fiduciary of the taxpayers of the district.
  • Our schools make Wheaton a community in which we can take great pride.

My Plan as a School Board Member

  • To continue to develop programs to help our students achieve and to prepare them for their post-secondary pursuits
  • To look for ways to support students who need extra help
  • To deliver our programs while maintaining a balanced budget
  • To continue a supportive and respectful relationship between staff and administrators within our district
  • To seek all avenues of personal education to be able to lead with
  • To facilitate the flow of information and feedback between the school district and the community
