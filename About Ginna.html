Ginna Ericksen, Candidate for Board of Education

About Ginna

I have been a stay-at-home mom and "career volunteer", dedicating my time and talents to causes that impact children. I have experience building relationships with children, teachers, staff and administration in the schools my children have attended. I have assumed leadership roles in both educational and community organizations. As my own children were coming to the end of their time in D200 I obtained my Masters in Counseling with a School Counseling specialty in order to obtain the credentials to continue working with young people. I have interned and worked as a Long-Term Substitute Guidance Counselor at Waubonsie Valley High School in D204.
